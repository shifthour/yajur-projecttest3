Company Overview

Second-generation, family-operated commercial grounds management firm exclusively serving commercial and institutional clients since 1988.

LSI operates 3 offices across Tennessee with regional reach throughout the Southeastern U.S., delivering both recurring maintenance contracts and project-based landscape construction.

100% Commercial Focus: Exclusively serves commercial and institutional clients across 24 end markets, including HOAs, healthcare, and corporate campuses.

Dual Revenue Streams: Combines stable, recurring grounds maintenance contracts with high-growth, project-based landscape construction.

Southeastern U.S. Platform: Operates from a Nashville, TN HQ with 3 offices, providing a strong regional platform for expansion and M&A integration.

Grounds Services

Recurring maintenance contracts including landscape management, tree care, seasonal color, and water management.

Landscape Construction

Project-based revenue from landscape/hardscape development, irrigation installation, and erosion control.
